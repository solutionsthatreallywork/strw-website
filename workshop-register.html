About This Workshop

Why Do We Keep Getting in Our Own Way?

Many people continue repeating painful relationship patterns — even when they genuinely want something different. They meet the same kind of person, have the same arguments, hit the same walls. And no matter how much they read, reflect, or try to change, something keeps pulling them back.

This workshop helps you understand why those patterns continue — not at a surface level, but at the deeper emotional and psychological level where they actually live. Once you can see the root, you can begin to change it.

In 90 focused minutes, Gary Jay walks you through the frameworks that explain why you've been working against yourself — and introduces the practical tools of Solutions That Really Work™ to help you stop.

Session Curriculum

What You'll Learn

Each topic builds directly on the last, giving you a complete picture of what has been keeping you stuck — and what to do about it.

Hidden Roadblocks

Identify the invisible emotional barriers formed long before adulthood that quietly sabotage your closest relationships — without you even knowing they're there.

Your Two Minds

Understand how your conscious intentions and your unconscious programming are often working in opposite directions — and what it takes to get them aligned.

Relationship Thermostat

Discover why you keep returning to the same emotional "set point" in relationships — and the step-by-step process for resetting it at the source.

Internal GPS (RAS)

Learn how your brain's Reticular Activating System filters what you notice — and how to reprogram it to guide you toward healthier, more fulfilling connections.

Stop Working Against Yourself™

Bring it all together with the core STRW framework — a practical, inside-out approach to lasting change in your relationships and every area of your life.

Practical Strategies

Leave with specific, actionable tools you can use immediately — not theory, not vague advice, but clear steps matched to real patterns and real situations.

The STRW Difference

Why This Isn't Like Other Workshops

Most relationship advice focuses on what to do — communicate better, set firmer boundaries, choose differently next time. That kind of advice is useful, but it rarely sticks. Why? Because it doesn't address the deeper programming that is driving your behavior in the first place.

Solutions That Really Work™ goes further. It teaches you why you repeat patterns — the hidden emotional wiring, the unconscious set points, the internal GPS that keeps navigating you back to familiar territory even when that territory is painful. Once you understand the root cause, lasting change becomes not just possible but natural.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?
Yes — completely free. There is no charge to register or attend. The companion workbook is also provided at no cost as part of the live session.
How long is the workshop?
The workshop lasts approximately 90 minutes (7:00–8:30 PM Eastern) and includes time for questions and discussion.
Do I need Zoom?
Yes. The workshop is hosted on Zoom. You can join from a computer, tablet, or smartphone — the Zoom app is free to download. You will receive a link and joining instructions by email after you register.
Is this therapy?
No. This is an educational coaching workshop, not therapy. Gary shares frameworks and practical strategies to help you understand your patterns and make intentional changes. If you are looking for ongoing therapeutic support, please work with a licensed mental health professional.
Is this workshop interactive?
Yes. Gary presents the material live and welcomes questions throughout. There is dedicated time for open Q&A at the end. You are welcome to participate as much or as little as you feel comfortable. Questions are encouraged, but never required.
Who is this workshop for?
This workshop is for adults who want to understand why they repeat patterns in relationships — whether they are looking for a relationship, currently in one, healing after one, or simply tired of feeling stuck. It is open to everyone regardless of age, background, or relationship status. No prior experience with coaching or psychology is required.
What should I have ready before the workshop?
All you need is a device with Zoom installed, a quiet space where you can listen comfortably, and an open mind. The companion workbook will be provided during the session itself — there is nothing to download or prepare in advance. Optionally, you may want to have a pen nearby to take notes alongside the workbook.
