Relationships & Connection

A Healthier Relationship With Others Begins Within Yourself

Relationships can bring love, intimacy, meaning, and connection. They can also bring confusion, conflict, fear, disappointment, and pain. Relationship coaching with Gary Jay helps you explore the hidden roadblocks that interfere with healthy connection — both with yourself and with others.

"Relationships don't complete you. They complicate you."

— Gary Jay

Many people look to relationships to feel complete, worthy, safe, or loved. But a relationship cannot do the work that must first begin inside yourself. When you understand your own feelings, needs, fears, patterns, and boundaries, you are better able to connect with others from a place of clarity instead of fear, need, anger, or self-doubt.

Fulfilling Connections With Yourself May Include

  • A healthier sense of self-worth and self-respect
  • The ability to like, trust, and take better care of yourself
  • A kinder and more respectful relationship with yourself
  • Greater awareness of your feelings, thoughts, and actions
  • More inner peace, confidence, and emotional stability

Fulfilling Connections With Others May Include

  • Greater trust, honesty, and emotional safety
  • Healthier communication and boundaries
  • More satisfying intimacy and connection
  • Less repeating of painful relationship patterns
  • More respect for yourself and the people in your life
  • A clearer understanding of when to move closer, set limits, or let go
What Coaching Can Help Achieve

When You Are Your Own Best Friend

When you are your own best friend, and truly love and respect yourself, then you can share yourself with another person who has done the same. Then you can just keep loving one another, be each other's best friend during good and challenging times, share life together, and have tremendous intimacy.

"Relationships do not fill you up with love. The work begins with learning how to build that love and respect within yourself." — Gary Jay
Three Areas of Relationship Coaching

Which Situation Fits You?

01
Preparing for a Healthy Relationship

For people who want to better understand themselves before entering or re-entering a relationship. Coaching can help you clarify what you are looking for, strengthen your relationship with yourself, develop healthier boundaries, and recognize patterns that may interfere with choosing or sustaining a healthy relationship.

02
Strengthening a Current Relationship

For people who are in a relationship and want to build greater trust, communication, intimacy, honesty, and emotional connection. Coaching can help you explore the patterns that show up in dating, commitment, marriage, family life, and friendship — and what changes may help the relationship become healthier and more fulfilling.

03
Healing After a Relationship Ends

For people working through the pain of a breakup, betrayal, divorce, loss, or the ending of an important relationship. Coaching can help you understand the emotional roadblocks that may keep you stuck in grief, guilt, anger, regret, or self-blame, while developing tools to heal, learn, and move forward with greater self-awareness and strength.
