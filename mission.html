Solutions That Really Work™

Our Mission

Helping you get, hold onto, and enjoy more pleasure, satisfaction, and success in every area of your life.

Get It

Many people struggle to get what they want — held back by fear, self-doubt, old patterns, or a belief that what they want isn't possible for them.

Hold Onto It

Others get what they want but cannot hold onto it. Success slips away, relationships deteriorate, or progress reverses — often for reasons that aren't obvious from inside the situation.

Enjoy It

Still others get what they want, hold onto it, and still suffer because they cannot fully enjoy it. Stress, guilt, anxiety, and hidden patterns rob them of the satisfaction they've earned.

The Mission

Helping People Stop Working Against Themselves

At Solutions That Really Work, our mission is to help people stop working against themselves by uncovering the hidden roadblocks that interfere with success, satisfaction, relationships, health, confidence, and emotional freedom.

It is not enough to simply motivate people. Real change requires inspiration, self-awareness, courage, and practical tools that help people move from where they are now to where they want to be.

"It's not enough to motivate, you need to inspire. And you do that by sharing your struggles and how you overcame them. Let the people connect to you and be inspired."

— Gary Jay
Gary's Journey

Professional Experience and Personal Transformation

Gary Jay's work is shaped by both professional experience and personal transformation. For more than four decades, Gary has been committed to helping children, adults, individuals, groups, and organizations create meaningful change.

Gary is not only a trained and experienced professional. He is also someone who understands, from personal experience, how hidden roadblocks can interfere with confidence, work, relationships, finances, emotional well-being, and the ability to fully enjoy life.

Through his own growth and professional work, Gary learned that real change requires more than motivation. It requires honesty, self-awareness, courage, practical tools, and the willingness to identify the patterns that keep people working against themselves.

Who the Mission Serves

Children, Adults, Families, and Organizations

Gary's mission includes helping young people develop self-awareness, emotional strength, focus, resilience, and confidence, while also supporting adults, schools, families, groups, and organizations in creating meaningful change.

Whether working one-to-one, with small groups, or with larger organizations, Gary helps people identify the small changes that can make the biggest difference in their lives.

The Solution

Stop Working Against Yourself

The solution begins with learning how to stop working against yourself. That means uncovering the hidden and not-so-hidden roadblocks that interfere with progress, confidence, relationships, satisfaction, and success.

Once those roadblocks are identified, they can be understood, worked through, removed, or approached in a new way. This is the mission of Solutions That Really Work: to help people get what they want in life, hold onto what matters, and truly enjoy more pleasure, satisfaction, and success by uncovering the roadblocks and applying solutions that really work.
