About Gary's Events

Real Tools Delivered in a Powerful Format

Through personal story, emotional honesty, practical psychology, humor, music, and real-world tools, Gary helps audiences uncover hidden roadblocks and stop working against themselves. Events and workshops may include personal growth presentations, relationship workshops, career and success programs, business coaching events, school-based programs, staff development, and customized experiences for organizations and community groups.

No public events are currently scheduled. To inquire about future events, private workshops, school programs, staff development, business presentations, or organizational trainings, please connect with Gary directly using the form below. Gary can also help you choose the topic and format that will create the greatest impact for your audience.

Event Topics

What Events & Workshops Cover

01
How to Get, Hold Onto, and Enjoy What You Want in Life

A live or virtual experience focused on uncovering hidden roadblocks and taking practical steps toward greater pleasure, satisfaction, success, and emotional freedom. Suitable for general audiences, organizations, and corporate events.

02
Fulfilling Relationships With Yourself and Others

A workshop experience focused on self-awareness, boundaries, emotional patterns, communication, and more meaningful connections. Suitable for individuals, couples, and organizations wanting to improve team dynamics and interpersonal communication.

03
Career, Financial, and Business Success

A practical growth experience focused on confidence, direction, work, leadership, money habits, business growth, and the roadblocks that interfere with success. Suitable for professionals, entrepreneurs, and organizations.

04
Train Your Brain for Focus, Confidence, and Change

A school-based or group workshop experience focused on self-awareness, emotional strength, focus, resilience, and practical tools for personal growth. Designed for students, young adults, and school communities.
